# Base Material

- chemical_composition: Mo-80% Ni [Page 2, Introduction].
- microstructural_properties: RF sputtered intermetallic alloy phase [Page 2, Introduction; Page 3, Experimental].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Non-nitrided MoNi4 thin film [Page 5, Table 3].
- manufacturing_process: Made by radio frequency (RF) sputtering a multiple composition target onto polished pure molybdenum substrate samples. Base pressure ~ 7 x 10^-7 torr. Argon gas pressure of 3 x 10^-2 torr. Forward power ~ 80 W. Film thickness ~ 2 µm [Page 2-3, Sample Preparation].
- heat_treatment_parameters: Heated to 150°C during deposition to improve adhesion [Page 3, Sample Preparation].
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Cathodic pretreatment performed in 0.1 M HCl for 15 min at -700 mVSCE to remove the air-formed oxide film [Page 3, Electrochemical Polarization].

- reference_electrode: Saturated Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 3, Electrochemical Polarization].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Nitrided MoNi4 thin film [Page 5, Table 3].
- manufacturing_process: Made by radio frequency (RF) sputtering a multiple composition target onto polished pure molybdenum substrate samples. Base pressure ~ 7 x 10^-7 torr. Argon gas pressure of 3 x 10^-2 torr. Forward power ~ 80 W. Film thickness ~ 2 µm [Page 2-3, Sample Preparation].
- heat_treatment_parameters: Heated to 150°C during deposition to improve adhesion [Page 3, Sample Preparation].
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Cathodic pretreatment performed in 0.1 M HCl for 15 min at -700 mVSCE to remove air-formed oxide film. Subsequently subjected to electrochemical surface nitriding in deaerated 0.1 M HCl + 0.5 M NaNO3, where samples were polarized at -700 mVSCE until 750 mcoul of charge had passed, then flushed with 0.1 M HCl [Page 3-4, Surface Nitriding].

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Potentiodynamic polarization testing from OCP in deaerated acidic solution to evaluate the influence of surface nitriding on passivation characteristics [Page 4, Surface Nitriding].
- pitting_potential: Not specified in text / No pitting observed (indicated as "-" in Table 3) [Page 5, Table 3].
- electrolyte_temperature: Not specified in text.
- polarization_scan_rate: 1 mV/s [Page 3, Electrochemical Polarization].
- reference_electrode: Saturated Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 3, Electrochemical Polarization].
- electrolyte_composition: 0.1 M HCl [Page 4, Surface Nitriding; Page 5, Table 3].
- aeration_status: Deaerated with high-purity argon for at least 2 h before analysis [Page 3, Electrochemical Polarization; Page 4, Surface Nitriding].
