# Base Material
- material_designation_and_classification: High-Entropy Alloy (HEA) / Medium Entropy Alloy, CoCrFeNi2, designated as alloy A35 [Page 1, Abstract; Page 2, Section 2.1].
- chemical_composition: 19.98 wt.% Fe, 40.84 wt.% Ni, 20.67 wt.% Co, 18.4 wt.% Cr. Trace elements: 11 ppm O, 11 ppm N, 165 ppm C, 10 ppm S [Page 3, Table 1].
- microstructural_properties: Single-phase face-centered cubic (FCC) solid solution microstructure. Equiaxed grain structure indicating full recrystallization. Average grain size is 40 µm (std. dev.: 4.8) with small inclusions from casting. ASTM grain size 6 [Page 3, Table 1; Page 4, Section 3.1].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: A35
- manufacturing_process: Formulated using commercial purity starting materials and in-house refined Ni-Co-Cr master alloys. Induction-melted under inert gas and poured with a 50°C superheat into a 75 mm cylindrical graphite mold having a nonreactive ceramic wash coat. Hot worked at 900°C consisting of forging and rolling to reduce the round ingots into slab shapes, ultimately formed into strip product with a thickness of approximately 3.7 mm [Page 2-3, Section 2.1]. Corrosion specimens were machined to 15 mm × 10 mm × 3 mm [Page 4, Section 2.3].
- heat_treatment_parameters: Given a computationally optimized homogenization heat treatment to reduce inhomogeneity to ± 1% of nominal or better. Ingots were preheated for 3 hours prior to fabrication [Page 3, Section 2.1].
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Bare corrosion specimens were wet ground through a 600-grit finish using SiC grinding paper, degreased with acetone, and air dried prior to testing [Page 4, Section 2.3].

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Potentiodynamic polarization measurements to evaluate the anodic electrochemical behavior and determine susceptibility to general and pitting corrosion. Specimen was kept at 800 mV more negative than the free potential for 2 minutes, and open circuit potential (Eoc) was measured for 30 minutes prior to sweeping [Page 4, Section 2.3].
- pitting_potential: 0.32 V versus SCE (breakdown potential, Ebre) [Page 7, Table 2].
- electrolyte_temperature: 25°C [Page 3, Section 2.3].
- polarization_scan_rate: Fixed voltage scan rate of 1 mV/s from an initial potential of −0.25 V versus Eoc to a final potential of 1.6 V versus Eoc [Page 4, Section 2.3].
- reference_electrode: Standard Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 3-4, Section 2.3].
- electrolyte_composition: 3.5 wt.% NaCl solution. Initial pH was 8.4; final pH of the solution was measured as 10.9 [Page 3, Section 2.3; Page 4-5, Section 3.2].
- aeration_status: Nondeaerated [Page 3, Section 2.3].
