# Base Material
- material_designation_and_classification: Equiatomic FeSiBAlNi high-entropy alloy (HEA) [Page 1, Abstract & Section 1].
- chemical_composition: Equiatomic Fe, Si, B, Al, and Ni (implied ~20 at% for each element) [Page 1, Abstract & Section 2].
- microstructural_properties: The as-milled powders were fully amorphous. After spark plasma sintering (SPS) at 520°C, the alloy exhibited an amorphous composite structure comprising simple body-centered cubic (bcc1) and face-centered cubic (fcc1) solid-solution phases. After SPS at 1080°C, the amorphous phase disappeared, the material was fully crystallized, and a new fcc phase (fcc2) appeared, resulting in a mixture of two fcc phases, one bcc phase, and some inevitable oxides [Page 1, Abstract; Page 3, Section 3.2; Page 4, Section 3.3].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: FeSiBAlNi HEA sintered at 520°C [Page 4, Section 3.4].
- manufacturing_process: Elemental powders were mechanically alloyed (MA) in a planetary ball mill at 350 r/min for 240 h under an Ar atmosphere with a ball-to-powder mass ratio of 15:1. The as-milled powders were subsequently consolidated by spark plasma sintering (SPS) in a graphite die under an axial pressure of 80 MPa in an Ar atmosphere [Page 2, Section 2].
- heat_treatment_parameters: Spark plasma sintered (SPS) at 520°C for 10 min at a heating rate of 40°C/min, followed by free cooling to room temperature [Page 2, Section 2].
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Ground using 2000-grit SiC paper to produce a smooth surface [Page 2, Section 2].

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Potentiodynamic polarization measurements were conducted in a three-electrode cell to evaluate corrosion behavior in a seawater solution after the open-circuit potential had sufficiently stabilized [Page 2, Section 2; Page 4, Section 3.4].
- pitting_potential: -0.56 V vs. Ag/AgCl [Page 4, Section 3.4].
- electrolyte_temperature: Room temperature (explicitly stated for immersion tests and inferred for the electrochemical measurements) [Page 2, Section 2].
- polarization_scan_rate: 3 mV/s [Page 2, Section 2].
- reference_electrode: Ag/AgCl reference electrode [Page 2, Section 2].
- electrolyte_composition: Seawater solution prepared by dissolving artificial sea salt in distilled water at a mass ratio of 1:30 [Page 2, Section 2].
