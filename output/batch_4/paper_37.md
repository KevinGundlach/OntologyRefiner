# Base Material
- material_designation: Experimental austenitic stainless steels, ~20Cr-25Ni-0Mo (Mo-free K series) [Page 7, Section 2.1]
- bulk_chemical_composition: [Page 7, Table 1]
  - K0: C 0.003, Si 0.53, Mn 1.31, P 0.010, S <0.003, Cr 20.14, Ni 24.75, Mo 0.08, N 0.010 (wt%)
  - K1: C 0.014, Si 0.53, Mn 1.31, P 0.010, S <0.003, Cr 20.14, Ni 24.75, Mo 0.08, N 0.064 (wt%)
  - K2: C 0.014, Si 0.55, Mn 1.43, P 0.009, S <0.003, Cr 20.03, Ni 24.87, Mo 0.04, N 0.130 (wt%)
  - K3: C 0.013, Si 0.55, Mn 1.43, P 0.010, S <0.003, Cr 19.96, Ni 24.90, Mo 0.04, N 0.180 (wt%)
- microstructural_properties: Austenitic structure, free from second phase precipitates, with a final grain size of 60–70 µm. Single-phase homogenous structure. [Page 7, Section 2.1 & Page 9, Section 3]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: K0, K1, K2, K3 (K series alloys)
- manufacturing_process: Produced as laboratory heats, then forged, hot rolled, and cold rolled to produce 3 mm strip. The low nitrogen content in K0 was attained by denitriding in hydrogen for 72 h at 1200°C, followed by cold rolling to reduce the grain size prior to final annealing. [Page 7, Section 2.1]
- heat_treatment_parameters: Annealed for 20 min at 1100°C and brine-quenched. [Page 7, Section 2.1]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Wet surface ground to 600 mesh SiC paper. Specimens were allowed to passivate in air for at least 18 h prior to testing. A combination of passivation and lacquering was used to restrict the exposed area and avoid crevice corrosion. [Page 7, Section 2.1 & Page 8, Section 2.2]
- exposed_surface_area: 1 cm² of the flat surface. [Page 8, Section 2.2]

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Potentiodynamic polarization testing to determine the final breakdown pitting potential (Eb) at 40°C. [Page 8, Section 2.2 & Page 11, Figure 3]
- pitting_potential: Final breakdown potential (Eb) estimates [Page 11, Figure 3, open points]:
  - K0 (~0.01% N): ~300 mV SCE
  - K1 (~0.06% N): ~400 mV SCE
  - K2 (~0.13% N): ~500 mV SCE
  - K3 (~0.18% N): ~600 mV SCE
- electrolyte_temperature: 40 °C [Page 11, Figure 3]
- polarization_scan_rate: 20 mV/min [Page 8, Section 2.2]
- reference_electrode: Saturated Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 8, Section 2.2]
- electrolyte_composition: 1 M NaCl [Page 8, Section 2.2]
- aeration_status: Not explicitly specified in the text for this specific test (though passive/active tests in Section 2.3 were deaerated with argon for 16 h). [Page 8, Section 2.2 & 2.3]
- corrosion_potential: Not specified in text [Page 8, Section 2.2 mentions scans started "from the corrosion potential" but no specific values are provided].
- ocp_stabilization_protocol: Not specified in text (specimens were allowed to passivate in air for at least 18 h prior to testing, but in-solution stabilization time is unstated). [Page 7, Section 2.1]
- counter_electrode_details: Not specified in text.
