- Chemical_Composition_and_Metrics: 0.055 wt% C, 1.53 wt% Mn, 0.006 wt% P, 0.014 wt% S, 1.01 wt% Si, 0.09 wt% Cu, 8.83 wt% Ni, 17.3 wt% Cr, 0.019 wt% Mo, 0.039 wt% N, 0.007 wt% Al [Page 2, Table 1]
- Microstructural_Features: Duplex microstructure (austenite and ferrite) with < 1.0% delta-ferrite content. No evidence of a sigma phase during x-ray examination [Page 2, Table 1 & Materials and Experimental Work]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Alloy 1 (Annealed) [Page 2, Table 1]
- manufacturing_process: Made in a 500 lb (227 kg) vacuum furnace. Ingots hot rolled down to a 0.5-in. thick (1.27-cm) plate and then cold rolled to a 0.25-in.-thick (0.64-cm) plate. Machined from annealed stock into cylinders 0.25-in. diameter by 0.75-in. long [Pages 1-2, Materials and Experimental Work]
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Solution annealed at 2000 F (1092 C) for 60 min and quenched in flowing water [Page 2, Materials and Experimental Work]
- surface_preparation_and_treatment: Polished to a 600 grit SiC finish. Degreased in an ultrasonic cleaner, pickled in 25 vol% HNO3/4 vol% HF at 140 F (60 C) for 5 min, washed in distilled water (7.6 megohm-cm at 25 C), and hot air dried. Mounted on a Teflon compression gasket and painted at the Teflon/steel interface with an alkyd varnish to avoid crevice corrosion [Page 2, Materials and Experimental Work]
- exposed_surface_area: 5 cm^2 [Page 3, Figure 2]
