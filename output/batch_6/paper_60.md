# Base Material
- material_designation: Al90Fe5Gd5, Al-transition metal-rare earth (Al-TM-RE) alloy [Page 1, Experimental]
- bulk_chemical_composition: 90 at% Al, 5 at% Fe, 5 at% Gd [Page 1, Experimental]
- microstructural_properties: Completely amorphous in the as-solidified condition. When partially crystallized, contains 10-15 nm diameter fcc Al nanocrystals (>98 at% Al) embedded in an amorphous matrix. When fully microcrystalline, consists of Al-rich fcc microcrystals depleted of TM and RE, which collect in intermetallic phases. [Pages 1-2, Experimental]
