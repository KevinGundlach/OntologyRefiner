# Base Material
- material_designation: Fe-24Cr, iron-chromium model alloy, ferritic stainless steel base [Page 1, Abstract; Page 2, Table 1].
- bulk_chemical_composition: Cr: 24.3 wt% (25.6 at%), Mo: 0.063 wt%, Si: 0.043 wt%, Mn: 0.002 wt%, C: 0.0033 wt%, S: 0.006 wt%, Fe: Balance (Data furnished by supplier) [Page 2, Table 1].
- microstructural_properties: Single phase with grain size in the millimeter range. Contains a few chromium oxide inclusions [Page 2, Experimental Method].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Fe-24Cr rotating disk electrode [Page 2, Experimental Method].
- manufacturing_process: Machined in the form of rods from which rotating disk electrodes were fabricated. The disk electrode was embedded in epoxy for polarization curve determinations [Page 2, Experimental Method].
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Heat treated at 1300°C for 58 h to homogenize the elemental concentration [Page 2, Experimental Method].
- surface_preparation_and_treatment: Mechanical polishing with SiC paper and with 1 μm alumina powder. Electrodes were washed with ethanol and doubly distilled water before being used [Page 2, Experimental Method].
- exposed_surface_area: 0.1 cm² (disk electrode of 3.57 mm diameter) [Page 2, Experimental Method].

- electrolyte_temperature: 65°C [Page 2, Experimental Method].

- electrolyte_composition: Electrolyte A: 0.1 M H₂SO₄ + 0.4 M Na₂SO₄ [Page 2, Experimental Method].
