# Base Material
- material_identification: Alloy 1, Super Duplex Stainless Steel (SDSS) [Page 4, Table 1]
- chemical_composition: 25.1 wt.% Cr, 7.1 wt.% Ni, 3.7 wt.% Mo, 3.8 wt.% Mn, 0.38 wt.% N, bal. Fe [Page 4, Table 1]
- microstructure_and_morphology: Duplex structure consisting of approximately equal volume fractions of austenite and ferrite phases [Page 2, Section 1; Page 5, Section 2.3]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Alloy 1, Solution Annealed at 1080°C
- manufacturing_process: Produced in a vacuum induction furnace (10 kg melt), followed by a homogenisation treatment at 1230°C for 10 h (in air), and forged between 1200°C and 1150°C into bars of dimension 15 × 65 mm² [Page 4, Section 2.1]
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Solution annealing at 1080°C (one-step treatment from 1300°C to 1050°C for 40 min, directly followed by water quenching) [Page 5, Section 2.2; Page 14, Section 3.4]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Mechanically ground with diamond paste (6 µm), slightly etched (5 s) in Blöch-Wedel II etching liquid (50 ml HCl techn. {37%}, 100 ml H2O and 1 g K2SO4), and rinsed with distilled water and ethanol [Page 7, Section 2.6]
- exposed_surface_area: 2 to 1000 µm diameter (glass capillary tip size of the microcell) [Page 7, Section 2.8]

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Microelectrochemical potentiodynamic polarization utilizing a glass microcapillary to evaluate the individual corrosion behavior of the single austenite and ferrite phases, as well as the phase boundary [Page 7, Section 2.8; Page 14, Fig. 8]
- pitting_potential: Not specified in text (microelectrochemical measurement of actual pitting potentials for single phases of SDSS at room temperature was deemed impossible due to extreme pitting resistance) [Page 9, Section 2.9]
- electrolyte_temperature: Room temperature, 23°C [Page 14, Fig. 8]
- polarization_scan_rate: 1 mV/s [Page 7, Section 2.6]
- reference_electrode: Calomel-electrode (SCE) [Page 7, Section 2.7]
- electrolyte_composition: 3 M HCl [Page 7, Section 2.6; Page 14, Fig. 8]
- aeration_status: Electrolytes were not aerated [Page 7, Section 2.6]
- corrosion_potential: ~ -380 mV SCE (estimated from Fig. 8) [Page 14, Fig. 8]
- ocp_stabilization_protocol: Cathodic prepolarisation performed for 2 min at -500 mV SCE prior to potential scan [Page 7, Section 2.6]
- counter_electrode_details: 0.5 mm thick platinum wire installed within the cell [Page 7, Section 2.8]
- electrolyte_ph: Highly acidic (3 M HCl) [Page 7, Section 2.8]
- passive_current_density: Austenite: ipass = 10 µA/cm²; Ferrite: ipass = 33 µA/cm² [Page 14, Section 3.4]
