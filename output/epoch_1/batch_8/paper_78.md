# Base Material
- material_identification: 25% Chromium Duplex Stainless Steel, designated as 3% Mo [Page 1, Abstract; Page 2, Experimental]
- chemical_composition: Fe-25.26% Cr-6.39% Ni-2.72% Mo-0.232% N (wt%), Pitting Resistance Equivalent (PRE) = 42 [Page 2, Table 1 and Experimental]
- microstructure_and_morphology: Austenite-ferrite duplex stainless steel [Page 1, Introduction]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Solution-annealed 3% Mo [Page 2, Experimental; Page 3, Results and Discussion]
- manufacturing_process: High-purity heats melted in a laboratory-scale vacuum induction furnace and cast in 25-kg ingots. Prepared in the form of cold-rolled sheets 3 mm thick and hot-rolled plates 12 mm thick. [Page 2, Experimental]
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Solution-annealed for 2 h at 1,050°C. [Page 2, Experimental]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Not specified in text for electrochemical testing. [Page 2, Experimental]
- exposed_surface_area: Not specified in text. [Page 2, Experimental]

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Anodic polarization testing to determine the pitting potential of the alloys in deaerated 4 M NaCl solution at 80°C. Conducted using a potentiostatic method in a 1-L multineck flask as specified in ASTM G 5. [Page 2, Experimental]
- pitting_potential: 609 mVSCE (average of five polarization tests) [Page 3, Results and Discussion]
- electrolyte_temperature: 80°C [Page 2, Experimental]
- polarization_scan_rate: Not specified in text for standard anodic polarization (Note: 1 mV/s is explicitly mentioned for the initial polarization step of the Critical Pitting Temperature test). [Page 2, Experimental]
- reference_electrode: Saturated calomel electrode (SCE) positioned in a salt bridge with a high-silica tip [Page 2, Experimental]
- electrolyte_composition: 4 M NaCl solution [Page 2, Experimental]
- aeration_status: Deaerated [Page 2, Experimental]
- corrosion_potential: ~ -630 mVSCE [Page 3, Results and Discussion and Figure 1]
- ocp_stabilization_protocol: Not specified in text. [Page 2, Experimental]
- counter_electrode_details: Platinum counter electrode [Page 2, Experimental]
- electrolyte_ph: Not specified in text. [Page 2, Experimental]
- passive_current_density: ~ 1 µA/cm² (estimated from the vertical passive region in Figure 1) [Page 3, Figure 1]
