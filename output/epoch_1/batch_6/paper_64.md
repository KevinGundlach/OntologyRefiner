## Conditioned Material

- manufacturing_process: 2 mm thick sheet cut into 10 mm circular discs. [Page 2, Experimental Procedures]
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Annealed (base material), ion implantation conducted at room temperature. [Page 2, Experimental Procedures]
