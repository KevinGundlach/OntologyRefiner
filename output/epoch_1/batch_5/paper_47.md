## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: Steel 1 (Duplex / Annealed) [Page 2, Table I]
- manufacturing_process: Melted in a 20-lb (9.2 kg) air induction furnace using low carbon ferroalloys and electrolytic metals. Hot rolled at 2200°F (1205°C) to 0.150-in. (3.8 mm) sheet. Air annealed at 1950°F (1070°C) for 15 min, pickled in 10 pct HNO3:2 pct HF at 150°F. Cold rolled 50 pct to 0.075-in. (1.9 mm). Annealed at 1950°F for 7 min in air, then pickled. Further pickled to remove 0.003 in. (0.075 mm) to eliminate the austenitized surface zone. [Page 2, METHODS AND PROCEDURE]
- thermal_treatment_parameters: Annealed at 1950°F (1070°C) for 7 minutes in air. [Page 2, METHODS AND PROCEDURE]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Degreased in a hot (120°F/50°C) concentrated solution of Alconox cleaner and thoroughly rinsed in water and alcohol. [Page 2, METHODS AND PROCEDURE]
- exposed_surface_area: 1 cm² exposed via a flat Teflon gasket with a 1/16 in. (1.6 mm) raised ring seal. [Page 3, Column 1]
