# Base Material
- material_designation_and_classification: Al0.5CoCrFeNi high-entropy alloy (HEAS) [Page 1, Title & Abstract].
- chemical_composition: Made from highly pure Al, Co, Ni, Cr, Fe, and Ni elements (99.99%) in a nominal Al0.5CoCrFeNi molar ratio [Page 1-2, Experimental procedures]. 
- microstructural_properties: The as-cast microstructure comprises an FCC solid solution matrix and droplet-shaped phases (Al–Ni rich phases). The aged microstructures comprise an FCC + BCC solid solution with a matrix, droplet-shaped phases (Al–Ni rich), wall-shaped phases (Al-depleted), and needle-shaped phases (Al–(Ni, Co, Cr, Fe)) [Page 1, Abstract; Page 2, Section 3.3].

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: As-cast Al0.5CoCrFeNi alloy [Page 1, Abstract]
- manufacturing_process: Melted under an Ar atmosphere in an arc furnace. Specimens with dimensions of 10 mm × 10 mm × 7 mm were prepared and retained in an as-cast condition [Page 1-2, Experimental procedures].
- heat_treatment_parameters: None (As-cast) [Page 2, Experimental procedures].
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Not specified in text.

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Electrochemical corrosion properties evaluated via potentiodynamic polarization curves when immersed in a 3.5% NaCl solution at room temperature, in accordance with the ASTM G59 test standard [Page 2, Experimental procedures].
- pitting_potential: 0.13 V (vs. SCE) [Page 6, Table 4].
- electrolyte_temperature: Room temperature / 25 °C [Page 2, Experimental procedures; Page 6, Table 4].
- polarization_scan_rate: Potentiodynamically scanned at a rate of 1 mV s⁻¹ from an initial potential of -0.5 VSCE with reference to the open circuit potential to a final potential of 1.5 VSCE [Page 2, Experimental procedures].
- reference_electrode: Saturated Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 2, Experimental procedures; Page 6, Table 4].
- electrolyte_composition: 3.5 wt% NaCl solution [Page 2, Experimental procedures; Page 7, Section 4].
- aeration_status: Not specified in text.
