# Base Material
- material_designation_and_classification: Al0.3CoCrFeNi High-Entropy Alloy (HEA) [Page 1, Title & Page 2, Section 2.1]
- chemical_composition: Atom ratios of Al/(CoCrFeNi) are 0.3/(1 + 1 + 1 + 1). Prepared by arc-melting a mixture of pure metals (99.99 wt.%). [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- microstructural_properties: Possesses a simple, single face-centered cubic (FCC) A1 phase structure with randomly-oriented equiaxed grains and elements distributed homogeneously in the solid solution. [Page 3, Section 3.1 & Page 4, Table 1]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: As-forged Al0.3CoCrFeNi [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- manufacturing_process: Cast alloy was prepared by arc-melting in a water-cooled copper hearth under a Ti-gettered argon atmosphere (remelted at least four times). The ingots were annealed, water quenched, and then forged with a reduction of 50% to a final thickness of about 10 mm, followed by air cooling. [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- heat_treatment_parameters: Before forging, annealed in a muffle furnace at 1250 °C for 50 h and water quenched. Forging occurred at 1250 °C, followed by air cooling. [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Specimens were cut to 1 × 1 × 0.5 cm³, cold-mounted in epoxy (exposed area of 1 cm²), ground with SiC papers from 200 to 2000 grit, and cleaned with alcohol, acetone, and deionized water. [Page 3, Section 2.4]

### Experiment
- experimental_test_summary: Potentiodynamic polarization test using a Potentiostat Workstation PARSTAT 2263 in a three-electrode electrochemical cell. Specimens were allowed to corrode freely for 30 min to reach open circuit potential (OCP), then scanned from -0.25 V vs. OCP until the current density reached 1 mA/cm². [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- pitting_potential: 522 (± 18) mVSCE [Page 9, Table 3]
- electrolyte_temperature: Room temperature (20 ± 2 °C) [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- polarization_scan_rate: 100 mV/min [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- reference_electrode: Saturated Calomel Electrode (SCE) [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- electrolyte_composition: 3.5 wt.% NaCl solution [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- aeration_status: Not explicitly specified in text (tests were conducted open to the air/naturally aerated). [Page 3, Section 2.4]

## Conditioned Material
- material_identifier: As-equilibrated Al0.3CoCrFeNi [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- manufacturing_process: Same casting and forging procedure as the "as-forged" condition, but subjected to an extended subsequent high-temperature equilibrium annealing process. [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- heat_treatment_parameters: After forging, samples were sealed in vacuum tubes and annealed in a muffle furnace at 1250 °C for 1000 h, then water quenched. [Page 2, Section 2.1]
- surface_preparation_and_finish: Specimens were cut to 1 × 1 × 0.5 cm³, cold-mounted in epoxy (exposed area of 1 cm²), ground with SiC papers from 200 to 2000 grit, and cleaned with alcohol, acetone, and deionized water. [Page 3, Section 2.4]

Specimens were allowed to corrode freely for 30 min to reach OCP, then scanned from -0.25 V vs. OCP until the current density reached 1 mA/cm². [Page 3, Section 2.4]
- pitting_potential: 808 (± 16) mVSCE [Page 9, Table 3]
